LECTURE 2, SEPTEMBER 19, 2016

Intro - Design with a Capital D

Addressing initial course feedback and concerns. Also, we examine the question of 'what makes a design good?'.

Assignment
Choose any object and write a one paragraph critique of it. Some questions to consider while doing this:

  • Who do you think it was designed for?
  • What's good?
  • What could be improved?
  • Include WHY (use principles from Lecture #2)
